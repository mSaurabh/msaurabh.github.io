About Me

I'm a software engineer with 15+ years of experience working across banking, consulting, energy, and insurance. As a Senior Software Engineer at Pariveda Solutions, I help teams work better together while building apps that solve real business problems.

What drives me is turning messy, complicated business challenges into clean, working solutions. I think differently than most people - I like to find new ways to solve old problems, even if it means taking some calculated risks along the way. My teams know they can count on me to speak up when something isn't working and to push through roadblocks until we get results.

I work best when I can dig into the technical details, spot patterns in data, and figure out how complex systems actually work. I'm the type of person who prefers to work independently, but I also know when to collaborate and when to teach others what I've learned. I say what I mean and mean what I say - no fluff, just straight talk about what needs to happen.
